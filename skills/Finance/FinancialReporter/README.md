# Financial Reporter Skill

## What Financial Reporter is

Financial Reporter is the finance skill for producing management-ready summaries from approved financial sources: revenue, expenses, cash flow, profit/loss, runway, variance, and KPI reports.

It explains what changed, why it may have changed, and what needs review.

## What Financial Reporter does

- Builds read-only financial summaries from approved data.
- Compares periods and flags variances/anomalies.
- Separates facts, estimates, assumptions, and recommendations.
- Produces owner/accountant-ready report notes.
- Keeps sensitive data private and aggregated when possible.

## When a bot should use it

Use Financial Reporter when the user asks for monthly reports, revenue/expense summaries, KPI dashboards, cash/runway summaries, or variance analysis.

Ask before sending reports externally or making financial, tax, legal, investment, or accounting-policy conclusions.

## Files in this skill

- [`INSTALL.md`](INSTALL.md) — verify report sources, periods, metrics, and read-only access.
- [`DOCTOR.md`](DOCTOR.md) — troubleshoot mismatched totals, missing data, metric definitions, and stale sources.
- [`PROMPTS.md`](PROMPTS.md) — user-facing prompts for financial reports.

## Golden operating rule

Financial reports must name source, period, currency, assumptions, and confidence. Do not hide missing data.
