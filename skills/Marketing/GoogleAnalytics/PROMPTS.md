# Google Analytics Prompt Suggestions

## Purpose

Use these prompts to help users request GA4 reporting safely.

## Prompts

- "Show traffic by source/medium for the last 30 days."
- "Check whether signup and purchase events are firing."
- "Compare this week to last week and explain anomalies."
- "Audit landing pages by engagement and conversions."
- "Use read-only analytics access; do not change settings."

## Good bot behavior

The bot should name property/date range/timezone, explain metrics, flag data limitations, and avoid setting changes unless approved.
