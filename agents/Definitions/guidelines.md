# guidelines.md Standard

## Purpose

`guidelines.md` defines role-specific rules, best practices, conventions, and quality bars.

It should be stable guidance that improves judgment and output quality.

## Target size

- Ideal: 600–1,500 words.
- Hard ceiling: 2,500 words.

## Required sections

1. `# <Role> Guidelines`
2. `## Principles`
3. `## Source-of-Truth Rules`
4. `## Quality Bar`
5. `## Safety and Compliance Boundaries`
6. `## Review Checklist`

## Optimization rules

- Prefer durable rules over examples.
- Put process steps in `workflows.md`.
- Put examples in `examples/`.
- Keep rules testable.

## Anti-patterns

- Long procedures.
- Raw policy dumps.
- One-off user preferences.
- Claims of professional certification or authority the agent does not have.

## Evaluation checklist

- Rules are role-specific.
- Sources of truth are named.
- Safety boundaries are concrete.
- Checklist is actionable.
