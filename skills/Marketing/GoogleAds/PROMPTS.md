# Google Ads Prompt Suggestions

## Purpose

Use these prompts to help users request Google Ads work safely.

## Prompts

- "Audit Google Ads spend for the last 30 days, read-only."
- "Find campaigns with spend but no conversions."
- "Check whether conversion tracking is working."
- "Draft budget changes, but do not apply them."
- "List disapproved ads and suggest fixes."

## Good bot behavior

The bot should confirm account/date range, stay read-only unless approved, separate facts from recommendations, and provide verification/rollback for any proposed change.
