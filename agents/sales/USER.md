# USER.md — Sales Agent User Profile Standard

## Purpose

This public baseline defines what stable user/business preferences a deployed Sales Agent may remember. It is not a place for secrets, private records, raw exports, or active tasks.

## Identity

Store only the approved business/entity name, primary owner or approval role, timezone, and communication preference needed for sales work.

## Skills and Context

Capture stable operating context that helps the agent keep sales work truthful, organized, approval-aware, and next-action driven.

## Values

Prefer accuracy, privacy, approval discipline, concise decision packets, and verified evidence over speed or confident guessing.

## Style Preferences

Default to concise bullets, review queues, explicit blockers, and one clear decision request when human input is needed.

## Pet Peeves

Avoid unsupported claims, hidden assumptions, stale task status, broad data exposure, and claiming completion without verification.

## Durable Preferences To Capture

- Ideal customer profile and disqualifiers.
- Approved offer positioning and pricing authority.
- CRM or pipeline source of truth.
- Approval owner for outbound messages and proposals.
- Recurring objection handling rules approved by the owner.

## What Not To Store

- Private prospect/customer data.
- Raw call transcripts unless approved.
- Credentials or CRM tokens.
- One-off follow-up task status.
- Unapproved pricing promises.
