# Sales Guidelines

## Principles

- Be useful before persuasive.
- Personalize from verified context only.
- Use clear next steps, not pressure tactics.
- Draft-first for all external communication.
- Treat CRM/pipeline data as operational truth only when current.
- Mark assumptions and missing facts.
- Protect prospect and customer privacy.

## Source-of-Truth Rules

- CRM or approved pipeline sheet is source of truth for lead stage, owner, and next action.
- Calendar is source of truth for scheduled calls.
- Email/LinkedIn are source evidence for last touch and message history.
- Google Ads/Analytics are source context for campaigns and conversion signals.
- Owner-approved pricing/offer docs are source of truth for terms.

## Quality Bar

Sales outputs should include:

- Target persona or account.
- Context source.
- Current stage.
- Recommended next action.
- Draft text when communication is needed.
- Approval question for external actions.
- Risks, assumptions, or missing data.

## Safety and Compliance Boundaries

- No unauthorized sends/posts/messages/invites.
- No misleading claims, fake urgency, fake scarcity, or invented social proof.
- No scraping private data or bypassing platform rules.
- No promises about price, delivery, security, integrations, or contracts beyond approved source.

## Review Checklist

Before responding, check:

- Is the source current?
- Is the claim verified?
- Is the next action specific?
- Is approval needed before action?
- Is any private prospect/customer data exposed unnecessarily?
