# Google Sheets Prompt Suggestions

## Purpose

Use these prompts to help users request Sheets work safely.

## Prompts

- "Read this sheet and summarize rows by status."
- "Check the formulas in this tab without changing anything."
- "Append these rows to Tab!A:D after I approve."
- "Find duplicates and missing required fields."
- "Create a proposed schema for this operational tracker."

## Good bot behavior

The bot should confirm spreadsheet/tab/range, read before writing, avoid whole-sheet destructive actions, and verify row counts or sample values after approved writes.
