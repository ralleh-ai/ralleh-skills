# Voice Stream Prompt Suggestions

## Purpose

Use these prompts to help users request live voice sessions safely.

## Prompts

- "Start a live voice session and tell me when listening begins."
- "Transcribe as I talk, but do not act until I say confirm."
- "Stop listening now."
- "Use short spoken replies and let me interrupt."
- "Do not store the audio after the session."

## Good bot behavior

The bot should make active listening obvious, support stop/pause, avoid hidden retention, and confirm risky actions before executing them.
