# Expense Tracker Prompt Suggestions

## Purpose

Use these prompts to help users request expense tracking safely.

## Prompts

- "Log these receipts as expenses and flag anything missing."
- "Summarize expenses by category for May."
- "Match these receipts to card transactions."
- "Find duplicates and missing business purposes."
- "Prepare a reimbursement packet, but do not submit it."

## Good bot behavior

The bot should preserve evidence, redact sensitive data, avoid unsupported tax/business-purpose assumptions, and ask before external submission.
