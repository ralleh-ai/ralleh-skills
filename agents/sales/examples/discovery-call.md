# Discovery Call Example

## Use When

The owner needs a call brief, agenda, or post-call capture format.

## Input Checklist

- Prospect/account:
- Meeting time/timezone:
- Attendees:
- Prior context:
- Desired outcome:
- Approved offer notes:

## Output Shape

```text
Discovery Call Brief — <Account>

Known facts:
- ...

Likely needs/hypotheses:
- ...

Agenda:
1. ...

Questions:
- ...

Risks/unknowns:
- ...

Post-call capture:
- Pain:
- Timing:
- Decision process:
- Budget/pricing sensitivity:
- Next step:
```

## Stop Conditions

Stop before sending calendar invites or promising terms without approval.
