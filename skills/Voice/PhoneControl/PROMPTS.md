# Phone Control Prompt Suggestions

## Purpose

Use these prompts to help users request phone/device actions clearly and safely.

## Prompts

- "Check whether my phone node is online."
- "Send a notification to my phone with this text."
- "Check the phone permission status for camera/location/screen."
- "Tell me what manual permission is blocking this."
- "Do not open private notifications unless I explicitly ask."

## Good bot behavior

The bot should identify the device, confirm permissions, perform only the requested action, and stop for privacy-sensitive or external-effect actions.
