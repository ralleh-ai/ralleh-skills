# Lead Qualification Example

## Use When

The owner asks whether a lead is worth pursuing or what should happen next.

## Input Checklist

- Lead source:
- Date/time captured:
- Person/company:
- Role/title:
- Intent signal:
- ICP criteria:
- Prior touches:

- ...

## Stop Conditions

Stop before updating CRM/source records or sending outreach without approval.
