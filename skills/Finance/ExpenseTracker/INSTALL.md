# Expense Tracker Install Guide

## Purpose

Use this file to verify safe expense tracking capability.

## Safety and scope

- Do not expose card numbers, bank data, tax IDs, or private receipts.
- Do not submit reimbursements or external reports without approval.
- Do not infer business purpose when unclear; ask or mark unknown.
- Keep source evidence linked or referenced.

## Prerequisites

- Expense source: receipts, invoices, card exports, accounting report, or user-provided list.
- Date range and currency.
- Category/project/client rules.
- Reimbursement or approval policy if relevant.

## Verify capability

1. Confirm source and date range.
2. Inspect sample fields or receipt metadata.
3. Confirm category rules.
4. Produce a read-only sample expense summary.
5. List missing decisions or evidence.

## Handoff prompt

> Verify expense source, period, currency, and categories. Create a read-only expense review with evidence links and flag missing receipts or unclear business purpose.
